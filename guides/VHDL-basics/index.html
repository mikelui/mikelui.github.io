Ordering in VHDL

Before we move on, letâ€™s distinguish one more difference between between traditional programming and HDLs. Traditional programming languages are based on the idea of computation sequences; in other words, steps are executed one after the other in the order they appear. In VHDL, we model a circuit, so data just propagates through wires.
